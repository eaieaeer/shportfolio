Why this exists

Testing shouldn't live on one person's laptop

Before
  • Regression tests run ad-hoc by one person
  • No shared visibility into what's covered
  • Results live in chat threads & screenshots
  • Hard to know if a service is healthy right now
After
  • Suites organized by service, open to the whole team
  • Same tests run for every new feature update
  • Runs are automated, scheduled, and logged
  • A live dashboard shows health at a glance
